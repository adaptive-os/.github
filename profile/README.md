# Welcome to AdaptiveOS

AdaptiveOS is a project dedicated to providing a **clean, performant, and privacy-focused desktop experience**. We believe in the power of Vanilla GNOME, the stability of Ubuntu, and the freedom of a Snap-free ecosystem.

## Our Philosophy
* **Vanilla GNOME:** Experience the desktop exactly as it was designed, without heavy, unnecessary modifications.
* **Snap-Free:** We believe in native packaging. AdaptiveOS is built from the ground up to prioritize Flatpak and native APT support.
* **Minimalist:** No bloat. Just the tools you need to get your work done.

## Our Architecture
We follow a modular, polyrepo approach:
* [iso](https://github.com/adaptive-os/iso): The heart of the project—build pipelines and configuration.
* [core](https://github.com/adaptive-os/core): System-level services and kernel configurations.
* [manager](https://github.com/adaptive-os/manager): Orchestration tools for the development environment.

## Get Involved
